SFL

This project features a logo designed for a local football tournament for Sudanese students attending INES-Ruhengeri in Musanze, Rwanda. The primary objective was to create a modern and strong visual identity for the event. To achieve this, I chose the Secretary bird—the national emblem of the Republic of Sudan—as the central motif, perfectly bridging the students' cultural heritage with the competitive spirit of the tournament.
